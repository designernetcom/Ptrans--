Flow Meters

Electromagnetic Flow Meters / Battery Operated Electromagnetic Flow Meters

Product Features
  • Measurement of flow rates of electrically conductive liquids such as water.
  • Available in Flanged and Wafer versions.
  • No obstructions and no pressure drop in liquid flow measurement.
  • Rubber, PTFE, Neoprene, Hard Rubber, and Polyurethane lined sensors.
  • Graphical LCD backlit or 2-line LCD backlit display options.
  • Excellent long-term accuracy.
  • Bi-directional flow measurement capability.
  • Empty pipe detection feature.
  • Selectable flow units for convenience.
  • Can be submerged in water or buried underground.
  • Explosion-proof (Ex-proof) version available.
Specifications
  • Power Supply : Universal 85-265 VAC, 45-65 Hz/ 24 VDC
  • Power Supply (for battery operated) 3.6V Battery Pack Max. 5 yrs life
  • Measuring Range (for battery operated) DN 15 - DN 1000
  • Measuring Range: DN15 - DN3000
  • Pressure: 6 / 10 / 16 kg/cm'
  • Minimum conductivity of measured liquid: 5µ5/cm
  • Electrode material: SS 316; Optional-SS 316L, Hastealloy C, Titanium or Tantalum
  • Bi-directional flow measurement capability.
  • Empty pipe detection feature.
  • Selectable flow units for convenience.
  • Can be submerged in water or buried underground.
  • Explosion-proof (Ex-proof) version available.
Output

Frequency, pulse and current outputs. Optional RS 232 and RS 485 outputs. Optional HART 0/P Optional GSM / GPRS connectivity

Protection

Remote Sensor - IP 68, Remote Converter - IP 67, Integral Sensor - IP 67
